Delicious Ivorian Menu

Experience the authentic taste of Côte d'Ivoire!

Appetizers

  • Alloco: Fried plantains served with spicy pepper sauce and fish.
  • Choukouya: Grilled meat (usually goat or beef) served with onions and spicy sauce.
  • Akara: Fried bean cakes made from black-eyed peas, onions, and spices.

Main Courses

  • Attiéké and Fish: A traditional Ivorian dish made from cassava, served with grilled fish and sauce.
  • Kedjenou: A delicious chicken or beef stew cooked in a sealed pot with vegetables and spices.
  • Garba: A popular street food made from Attiéké (cassava) served with fried fish and spicy sauce.

Desserts

  • Fruits au Sirop: Fresh Ivorian fruits soaked in syrup, served cold.
  • Banana Fritters: Deep-fried bananas coated in a light batter, served with a dusting of powdered sugar.
  • Yamoussoukro Cake: A traditional cake from the capital city, made with cassava flour and coconut.
